## Top Pick: Gut's Soul Yoyogi

**Best for**: Absolute cheapest unlimited yakiniku

- All-you-can-eat from ¥1,490 (chicken only)
- Full beef package at ¥2,490
- Near Yoyogi Station (close to Shibuya)
- **CASH ONLY**

## Runner Up: Yakiniku Like

**Best for**: Solo diners, quick meals, flexibility

- Personal grill stations
- Sets from ¥990
- Multiple Shibuya locations
- Tablet ordering with English
- Fast 3-minute service

## Best Value Wagyu: GYUMARU

**Best for**: Quality wagyu on a budget

- Matsuzaka beef at ¥1,980 average
- A5 Geta Kalbi at ¥1,580
- 8F Shibuya Jow Building

## Other Notable Options

### All-You-Can-Eat

- **Wagyu Palace Niku Yokocho Udagawacho** - Whole-cow purchasing keeps wagyu prices down
- **CHURRASCO GANG Shibuya** - Brazilian BBQ style, 5 min from station
- **BeBu-Ya Shibuya** - Wagyu AYCE with 90-min limit

### Lunch Deals

- **Ushigoro Bambina Shibuya** - A5 wagyu lunch sets (cheaper than dinner)
- **Yakiniku 29 Terrace Shibuya South** - Brand beef lunch specials
