# Gut's Soul Yoyogi

**The cheapest all-you-can-eat yakiniku in Tokyo.**

## Location

Few minutes walk from Yoyogi Station (Yamanote Line). On the 3rd floor right by the train tracks - window seats have views of passing trains.

## Pricing (All-You-Can-Eat)

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chicken only | ¥1,490 | Chicken, 90-120 min |
| Chicken + Pork | ¥1,990 | Chicken, pork, 90-120 min |
| Full Package | ¥2,490 | Chicken, pork, beef, sides, soft drinks |

Choose 90 or 120 minute sessions. Use electronic keypad to order plates.

## Hours

- **Mon-Fri Lunch**: 11:30-15:00
- **Mon-Fri Dinner**: 17:00-23:00 (Food LO 22:00)
- **Weekends/Holidays**: 15:00-23:00 (no lunch)

## Notes

- **CASH ONLY** - no card payment
- Avoid a-la-carte items (priced separately) to stay on budget
- Very popular with budget travelers
